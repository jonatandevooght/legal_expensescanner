# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 24/03/2026  
**App:** Snap Cart: AI Expense Scanner

## 1. Data We Collect
We may process the following data:
* Account information (such as email address, display name, user ID).
* Content you upload (such as receipts/photos).
* App usage data (such as error logs, device and session information).
* Subscription status (active/inactive) and payment status via external payment providers (Apple/Google/Superwall).

*Note: we generally do not receive full payment card details.*

## 2. Why We Use This Data
We process data to:
* operate the app (account, login, storage, sync);
* process receipts and show totals/statistics;
* manage subscriptions/paywalls;
* perform security and fraud prevention;
* provide support and improve the app.

## 3. Legal Basis (GDPR)
We rely on the following legal bases:
* performance of a contract (providing app functionality);
* legitimate interests (security, product improvement);
* legal obligations (where applicable);
* consent (where required, e.g., certain tracking or marketing).

## 4. Sharing With Third Parties
We only share data with service providers required to operate the app, such as:
* authentication/database/storage providers (e.g., Supabase),
* subscription and paywall services (e.g., Superwall),
* cloud/hosting and analytics providers (if used).

These parties process data on our behalf or as independent processors/controllers under their own terms.

## 5. Data Retention
We do not keep personal data longer than necessary:
* account data for as long as your account is active;
* receipts and related data as needed for app functionality, unless you request deletion;
* technical logs for a limited period for security/debugging purposes.

## 6. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have rights to:
* access, correct, or delete your data;
* restrict or object to processing;
* data portability;
* withdraw consent (where applicable).

## 7. Security
We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure.

## 8. Children
The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under that age.

## 9. International Transfers
Data may be processed outside your country/region. Where required, we apply appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses).

## 10. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or by other appropriate means.
